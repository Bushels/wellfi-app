- Strong EOR potential to reduce sustaining capital

## Cross-PDF Observations

1. **2024 vs 2025 Reserves:** The Corporate Presentation uses 2024 year-end reserves (pre-2025 report) showing PDP 85.3, 1P 151.1, 2P 221.2 Mmboe. The Reserves Release shows 2025 year-end post-disposition: PDP 54.2, 1P 104.8, 2P 158.4 Mmboe. The reduction is primarily from the Pembina disposition (53.7 Mmboe 1P, 75.3 Mmboe 2P removed).

2. **Peace River Locations Evolution:** Corporate Presentation (2024 year-end) shows 160 net 2P locations. Reserves Release (2025 year-end) shows 180 net (77 BS + 103 CW), representing +20 net new locations year-over-year.

3. **Belly River Emergence:** Only 14 booked 2P Belly River locations in WG at year-end 2025, but 12 were added in 2025 alone, with 5.6 Mmboe added. Strong economics (169% IRR, 0.7 yr payout) suggest rapid inventory growth ahead.

4. **Waterflood Strategy:** $22M allocated in 2026 (within $80M PR budget). Waterflood added 3.5 Mmboe on 2P basis in 2025. Target: 35% of Clearwater production waterflood-supported by end-2026.

5. **Well Design Terminology:** Heavy oil wells are described as "multi-lateral, open hole producers" (10-leg Bluesky, 8-leg Clearwater). No "fishbone" or "waffle" terminology found in these PDFs -- those may be informal industry terms for the multi-lateral designs or may appear in technical documents not included here.
